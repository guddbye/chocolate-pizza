For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chuncky-jammy mixture. Add salk with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a samll pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulate sugar until sguar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolk. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream macine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to the manufacturere instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for twenty minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- ten minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before servering.

1 1/2 cups of milk
1/2 cup of marscarpone
1/2 tsp. pink salt
1 lb. Black Mission Figs
1/2 cup of brown sugar
2-3 tbsp. water
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1/3 granulated sugar
2 eggs yolk
1 lemon, juiced
2 tbsp butter
1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly chopped
